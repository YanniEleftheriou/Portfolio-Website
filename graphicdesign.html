Graphic Design
Bots of Instagram
Bots of Instagram is a zine designed in InDesign and Illustrator spotlighting a niche internet subculture: pornographic spam bots that infiltrate social media comment sections. I mixed archival screenshots with a Mondrian-inspired layout to contrast clean modernist structure with chaotic cyber-automation. The design explores how these bots shape digital spaces, affect user experience, and blur the boundaries between authenticity and artificial interaction.
Advocacy Campaign
This poster was designed for a class assignment in which we created an advocacy campaign for a political or cultural issue. I focused on the Prespa Agreement and the showcasing Greek identity, using imagery of Alexander the Great and a visual style inspired by the film poster for Zorba the Greek. The poster uses bold composition and expressive typography to communicate a strong point of view. It was an exercise in poster design, cultural messaging, and visual storytelling.
Retrospective Prints
These collage prints were created for a photography final and produced in Photoshop. The compositions draw inspiration from Barbara Kruger and Yokoo Tadanori, blending appropriated media, personal photos, and bold graphic overlays. The pieces explore identity, memory, and visual excess through layered color and text. They stand out as expressive, experimental graphic prints.
Concrete Poem
This concrete poem reimagines the lyrics of “California” by Lana Del Rey using an X-shaped typographic form. The piece explores how layout, pacing, and spatial rhythm can shape the reading of a text. It was later added to an are.na collection for typographic inspiration, which was a nice recognition of its experimental style.
Data Visualization
This collection brings together several data-driven graphics created for my Information Aesthetics course at Purchase College. I conducted all of the research myself, gathering source material, compiling datasets, and organizing everything in Excel before visualizing the results in Adobe Illustrator. Projects include the Pursuit of Prosperity Dashboard, which compares country GDP, happiness index scores, suicide rates, and levels of social support, as well as a Purchase College Water Quality Survey that I designed using both student responses and environmental data I collected. Each visualization focuses on translating complex information into accessible and meaningful visual systems.
